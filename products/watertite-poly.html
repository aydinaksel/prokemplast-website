WATERTITE POLY

Two-Component Cementitious Waterproofing – Acrylic & Fiber Reinforced

Price: $150

Availability: In Stock

Contact to Order Email Sales

WATERTITE POLY is a two-component, cement-based, acrylic and fiber-reinforced waterproofing layer. Designed for use on decks, balconies, and terraces, it provides a UV-resistant, flexible base for tiling and effective positive-side waterproofing.

Product Composition

  • 20 kg Dry Mix
  • 10 kg Liquid Additive
  • Can be thinned with up to 10% water as needed

Key Features

  • Highly elastic and durable
  • UV and weather resistant
  • Great leveling properties
  • Excellent substrate adhesion
  • Provides a waterproof base for tile and stone applications

Application

  • Mix both components thoroughly using a low-speed mixer until smooth
  • Apply with trowel, squeegee, or brush in 2–3 mm thickness
  • Multiple coats recommended, applied in cross-directions
  • Use fiber mesh between layers for reinforcement
  • Open Time: 30 minutes
  • Application Temp: 8°C – 30°C
  • Curing Time: 7 days at 20–30°C

Coverage

  • Wet Rooms: 2.5–3 kg/m² (2 coats)
  • Terraces, Pools, Water Tanks: 3.5–4 kg/m² (2 coats)

Storage

12 months in dry conditions in unopened packaging.

Surface Preparation

  • Surfaces must be clean, dry, structurally sound, and free of contaminants
  • Repair cracks, holes, and surface defects prior to application

Safety & Handling

  • Warning: May cause skin, eye, or respiratory irritation
  • Precautions: Wear eye protection, gloves, and a respirator
  • First Aid: Rinse skin or eyes with water; seek medical attention if needed
  • Spills/Leaks: Avoid release into drains; maintain a clean workspace
  • Disposal: Dispose according to local regulations
  • Manual Handling: Use team lifting and back supports to prevent injury

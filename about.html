About Us

At Prokemplast, we’re dedicated to building a sustainable future through innovative, environmentally responsible construction products. As both a manufacturer and distributor, our mission is simple: to deliver high-performance solutions that are safe for people, environmentally protective, and cost-effective over their entire lifecycle.

Our products are thoughtfully engineered to meet the highest standards of safety and sustainability. We prioritize non-toxic, recyclable materials that safeguard both workers and building occupants while minimizing environmental impact. With a strong emphasis on energy efficiency, durability, and ease of installation, Prokemplast solutions are designed to lower energy use, reduce maintenance needs, and extend building longevity.

From lightweight, smart materials that cut transportation costs to innovations that improve indoor air quality and occupant comfort, every aspect of our work reflects our core values—health, sustainability, innovation, and long-term value. At Prokemplast, we don’t just create materials—we create better spaces for people to live, work, and thrive.

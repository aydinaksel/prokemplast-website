About Us

Prokemplast designs and distributes building materials that are innovative, high‑performance, and environmentally responsible. We combine practical job‑site experience with materials science to create solutions that build better, faster, and cleaner—without compromising safety or durability.

As both a manufacturer and distributor, our focus is simple: deliver measurable performance with a lower environmental footprint. From lighter formulations that reduce transport emissions to products engineered for thermal efficiency and long service life, our materials help contractors and owners achieve sustainable results that last.

What We Stand For

  • Innovation: Modern formulations that solve real job‑site problems.
  • Performance: Materials engineered for efficiency, strength, and longevity.
  • Environment: Safer chemistries and smarter use of resources.

Built For Real‑World Use

Every Prokemplast product is developed with installers in mind—easy to apply, consistent to work with, and proven in the field. The result is faster application, reduced waste, and comfortable, durable spaces for the people who use them.
